HOW IT WORKS?

How to buy and print a design?

Steps:

1. You can first start by choosing and clicking on the design that you want print on Triplaxis.
2. You can then choose the resolution of the printing. (100 ,200 and 300 microns)
3. Next you would have to choose the selection of colour for your printing.
4. Then you would need to choose the material for your printing.
5. The price estimation will be shown below. (Note: Amount, material type, resolution, price of design and size of design will affect the price.)
6. Finally after deciding on all options, you can proceed to add the items into your cart.
7. After you are done adding items into your cart, you can then proceed to the check out page and start printing your product.

How to print your own design?

1. Go to the "Print Your Own Design" page.
2. Upload your own design. (Supports .obj and .stl files only)
3. You can then choose the resolution of the printing. (100 ,200 and 300 microns)
4. Next you would have to choose the selection of colour for your printing.
5. Then you would need to choose the material for your printing.
6. The price estimation will be shown below. (Note: Amount, material type, resolution, price of design and size of design will affect the price.)
7. Finally after deciding on all options, you can proceed to add the items into your cart.
8. After you are done adding items into your cart, you can then proceed to the check out page and start printing your product.

How to sell you design and make money?

1. Go to the "Sell" page.
2. Enter your design's name.
3. Enter selling price.
4. Select your product's category.
5. Fill in the product's description. (Size in volume, use of product and more details.)
6. Upload the images of the actual product. (Front view, top down view ... )
7. Upload your design file. (*NOTE: ONLY .obj & .stl file formats*)
8. Proceed and wait for the money to roll in!
